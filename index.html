Installation

  1. Download the latest version of TurboUI from the Downloads section below.
  2. Boot your device into recovery mode. Use either TWRP or OrangeFox
  3. Follow the instrucions on the XDA page of your recovery
  4. Wipe cache and data partitions
  5. Install the downloaded ROM zip file.
  6. Reboot your device and enjoy TurboUI!

FAQ

Is rooting my device required?

No, rooting your Samsung Galaxy S20FE is not required to install and use TurboUI.

However you need an Unlocked Bootloader and a Custom Recovery installed

Is TurboUI Custom ROM compatible with other Samsung Galaxy models?

TurboUI is specifically designed for the 4G and 5G Snapdragon Samsung Galaxy S20FE SM-G781B and SM-G780G.

However there are occasional Exynos (SM-G780F) builds and we might even expand to other devices in the future.

Can I revert to the stock Samsung ROM after installing TurboUI?

Yes, you can revert to the stock Samsung ROM if you wish to do so.

However you need to understand that this will not revert your Knox status.
